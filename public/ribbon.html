* Commander's Commendation Award may be presented by a Wing, Region or National Commander:
  • Members who earn the award as authorized by the Wing Commander will be awarded the basic ribbon.
  • A bronze star is added to the ribbon when the award is authorized by the Region Commander.
  • A silver star is added to the ribbon when the award is authorized by the National Commander.
